Watch our Documentary

"Dosed with Humanity" is a student made documentary that shares three unique experiences of substance use. Our goal is to amplify overlooked voices and share the human side of substance use to support harm reduction efforts and foster de-stigmatization via providing a glimpse of humanity in each person. Watch this 18 minute documentary to join the fight against stigma.

Watch the Trailer

Not sure if you're interested? Take a quick second to watch our trailer and discover the stories we share and the lessons we have had the privilege of hearing. Please note: all components of the documentary and trailer were made by students apart of NaloxAware and their own personal equipment.
